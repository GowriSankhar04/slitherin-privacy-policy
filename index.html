Privacy Policy for Slitherin

Effective Date: 13 September 2025

Slitherin (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Slitherin, we may collect the following information:

  • Images: You may upload images of snakes for identification purposes.
  • Device Information: Basic technical information such as device type, operating system, and app version.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you use the app, including features accessed and duration of usage.

Note: We do not collect your name, email, location, or any other personal identifiers unless you explicitly provide them.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Identify snake species from uploaded images.
  • Improve app performance and enhance features.
  • Provide multilingual results for better user experience.
  • Analyze app usage to understand and improve our service.

Your images may be processed only within the app or on secure backend servers for species detection.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or images to third parties.

We may share information in the following cases:

  • With service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., cloud hosting for processing images).
  • When required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of the app, users, or others.

4. Data Storage and Security

Images uploaded to our app are processed securely and stored temporarily only if necessary for species detection.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

5. Children’s Privacy

Slitherin is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services (e.g., Google Play Services) to process images or improve functionality. These services may have their own privacy policies.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of any uploaded images.
  • Request information on what data is collected about you.
  • Withdraw consent for data usage at any time.
